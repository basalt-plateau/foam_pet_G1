Foam Pet Rules
Preface

Most likely every cyte + tissue + organ that makes up this pet is some kind of copyleft or unrestrictive.

Therefore, playing with, training, and cloning your pet is most likely 100% legit.

Essentially there is the "Foam Pet Docker Image", "Docker-Debian", "Debian", plus "Components". The rules of each is detailed below.

Details
Foam Pet Docker Image

This is a mix and a built from "docker-debian" plus "debian". Original parts of the mix are subject to the aggregate rules in the /Rules directory.

Docker Debian

https://github.com/jitesoft/docker-debian This is the docker image builder. The MIT License can be found at /Trinkets/docker-debian/LICENSE

Debian

"Debian" is a mix. https://debian.org The "Social Contract 1.2" plus the "why_debian" suggested that "Debian" is 100% free, and that others may distribute modified derivatives. https://debian.org/social_contract#guidelines https://debian.org/intro/why_debian "why_debian" has this text: "Debian is made of free and open source software and will always be 100% free. Free for anyone to use, modify, and distribute."

PyPI, foam_pet

The "foam_pet" python3 soft is a mix.

The "foam_pet" pypi original parts are GPL 3.0. "https://pypi.org/project/foam_pet"

The "foam_pet" frontend licenses can be found at: /habitat/v_env/lib/python3.11/site-packages/foam_pet/sveltnetics_build/frontend-licenses.txt

The "foam_pet" pip PyPI licenses can be found within each soft at: /habitat/v_env/lib/python3.11/site-packages
